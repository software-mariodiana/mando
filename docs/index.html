Master the Melody!

Each note sharpens your memory and challenges your musical prowess.

Rediscover the excitement of syncing to the rhythm of lights and sounds with Tōn Klōn, our modern tribute to the beloved memory games of yesteryear.

Pronounced "Tone Clone," Tōn Klōn beckons players from every generation to immerse themselves in a symphony of pulsating patterns and radiant colors. It's more than just a game; it's a nod to the classic era, revitalized for today's savvy players.

Why will Tōn Klōn captivate you?

  • Simple, yet compelling gameplay designed to sharpen your memory and test your quickness.
  • A symphony of harmonic tones paired with a spectrum of bright visuals for a full sensory experience.
  • A dynamic difficulty curve that adapts to keep you engaged and always ready for the next challenge.

Embark on a journey that blends the charm of retro with the flair of the modern. Stay tuned to our page for the latest updates, player tips, and community happenings. Embrace the legacy and forge new memories with Tōn Klōn!
