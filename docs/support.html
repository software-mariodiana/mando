Support

We'd Love to Hear from You!

Hey there, Tōn Klōn enthusiasts!

We're thrilled that you're enjoying the melodic challenges of Tōn Klōn. Our team is dedicated to providing a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone, and your feedback is music to our ears!

Encountered a Bug?

If you've come across a bug that's disrupting your groove, let us know! While Tōn Klōn is a labor of love offered freely to the world, we're always looking to improve. Describe the issue you encountered, and we'll look into it as soon as we can — no promises, but we aim to keep the tunes rolling smoothly.

Got a Suggestion?

Do you have ideas on how to make Tōn Klōn even more harmonious? Share your suggestions with us! While we can't implement every idea, we're open to inspiration that could enhance the symphony of fun that is Tōn Klōn.

To report a bug or share your thoughts, simply reach out to us at: Tōn Klōn Support.

Remember, Tōn Klōn is a free experience, and while we're passionate about making it the best it can be, our resources for updates and support are in rhythm with the beats of a hobby project. Your patience and understanding are appreciated as we balance our love for Tōn Klōn with the other melodies of life.

Thank you for your support and for being a part of the Tōn Klōn community!

Keep tapping to the rhythm!
